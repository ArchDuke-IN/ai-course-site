title: AI for Management & Research
description: A hands-on course teaching AI from a management perspective and research methodology.
---

This course demystifies artificial intelligence for a non-technical audience. We go light on math and heavy on intuition. The goal is for you to build an appreciation for how underlying AI models work and how they unlock value from data in practice. 

We will cover machine learning, generative models, prompting, agentic AI, and responsible use. The curriculum is divided into a **Shared Foundation**, a **Management Track** for leaders, and a **Research Track** for analysts and academics.

## Curriculum Tracks

### 1. Shared Foundation
The basics of AI, how generative models work, prompting, agentic AI, and responsible use. **Taken by everyone.**

- [Module F1.1: From Rules to Learning to Generation](/ai-course-site/foundation/f1-1-from-rules-to-learning-to-generation/)
- [Module F1.2: Prompting & Interacting with LLMs](/ai-course-site/foundation/f1-2-prompting-interacting-with-llms/)
- [Module F1.3: Agentic AI & Advanced Prompting](/ai-course-site/foundation/f1-3-agentic-ai-advanced-prompting/)

### 2. Management Track
For leaders, consultants, and functional managers who need the judgment to evaluate, adopt, and govern AI — not to build it.

- [Module M1.1: Intelligent Systems: What AI Actually Is for Business](/ai-course-site/management/m1-1-intelligent-systems-what-ai-actually-is-for-business/)
- [Module M2.1: The AI Value Chain & Economics](/ai-course-site/management/m2-1-the-ai-value-chain-economics/)

### 3. Research Track
For academics, doctoral students, and analysts who want to use AI as part of the research process and as a research method.

- [Module R1.1: Why AI Matters for Research Now](/ai-course-site/research/r1-1-why-ai-matters-for-research-now/)
- [Module R1.2: AI for Literature Review & Synthesis](/ai-course-site/research/r1-2-ai-for-literature-review-synthesis/)
- [Module R2.1: AI for Data Analysis & Coding](/ai-course-site/research/r2-1-ai-for-data-analysis-coding/)
- [Module R3.1: AI for Writing & Communication](/ai-course-site/research/r3-1-ai-for-writing-communication/)
